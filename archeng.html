ACOUSTIC SOLUTIONS FOR HOMES AND PREMISES

Common noise-related problems we solve:

Noise between neighbors (impact and/or airborne transmission)

· Footsteps, furniture dragging (through floors)

· Voices, TV, or music (poorly insulated or uninsulated walls)

Exterior noise (facades and windows)

· Traffic, construction work, or nightlife (weak window or wall insulation)

· Airborne noise from planes or trains in exposed areas

Noise from installations (inside and outside the building)

· Elevators, pipes, heat pumps, or HVAC systems

· Vibrations from mechanical equipment (boilers, ventilation)

Noise in renovations and refurbishments

· Acoustic upgrades in floors and walls to comply with current regulations (CTE DB-HR, municipal ordinances, Noise Law, etc.)

· Problems with partition walls between dwellings (inadequate partitions)

Noise in residential communities

· Conflicts caused by commercial units or gyms (solutions for mixed-use buildings)

· Compliance with Horizontal Property Law (acceptable noise limits)

ACOUSTIC CONDITIONING

· Improving acoustic conditions in hospitality venues (restaurants, cafés, bars, etc.) to prevent long-term exposure to harmful noise for staff and customers

· Echo and reverberation in large spaces (living rooms, basements, etc.)

· Sound treatment in recording studios or rehearsal rooms
